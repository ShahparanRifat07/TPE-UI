Factors

Counseling & Mentoring

Q1 - The teacher gives his students advice on how to make the most of both their class time and their free time.

Q2 - The teacher knows his students personally and enriches his courses by taking into consideration his studentsâ€™ individual differences. The teacher is creative.

Research & Development

Q1 - They bring new research projects to the institutions they belong to.

Q2 - The themes that they are researched are highly complex and are perceived as being a novelty in the field.

Organizational Qualities

Q1 - The teacher plans his activities and while implementing his activities, the teacher aims to take each of his students further than the current level of their knowledge.

Professional & Personal Development

Q1 - The teacher is selfless, committed to his work, and takes unorthodox decisions on the development of his students.

Q2 - The teacher is aware of his responsibilities for his courses.

Q3 - They convey enthusiasm for their field, both verbally and nonverbally.

Q4 - Monitors the extent of achievements of students, discusses and identifies the weaknesses for remediation.

Administrative skills

Q1 - They bring their contribution to the development of the department/faculty/university.

Q2 - They initiate study programs, postgraduate courses, training and development courses.

Q3 - Prepare the academic calendar reflecting all the activities to be undertaken by the teacher throughout in the teaching learning process.
